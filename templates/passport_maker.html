Correct Photo Sizes

India passport: 35x45mm (1.2"x1.4"), USA: 51x51mm (2"x2"), UK: 35x45mm. Always check official requirements before submission.

üí°

Lighting & Background

Use natural daylight, plain white or light blue background. Avoid shadows on face and background. Our AI removes background automatically.

üëî

Dress Code

Wear formal attire with contrast to background. Avoid white clothes on white background. No uniforms or hats (except religious).

üòä

Facial Expression

Neutral expression, both eyes open, mouth closed. Look directly at camera. Remove glasses unless worn daily for medical reasons.

Printing Quality

Quick Tips
